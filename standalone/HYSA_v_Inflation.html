Annual HYSA after-tax interest versus inflation erosion on $10,000, with net real return lines for Roth (0% tax) and a user-selected marginal rate.

Methodology: Top competitive HYSA rate × $10,000 × (1 − tax rate) − (CPI-U × $10,000) = net real gain/loss. "Roth" assumes 0% tax on interest (approximating a Roth IRA savings account).
HYSA rates: Top competitive rates estimated from FRED, FDIC data, and Fed funds rate history.
Inflation: BLS CPI-U annual averages.
Note: HYSA rates vary throughout the year; figures shown are annual averages of top offers. Inflation (CPI-U) also varies monthly; figures are calendar-year averages.
