Cumulative real purchasing power of $10,000 invested in a top-tier HYSA, adjusted for inflation and taxes, from the selected start year through 2025.

Methodology: Each year, interest = HYSA rate × balance. After-tax interest = interest × (1 − tax rate). Inflation adjustment = balance × CPI rate. Net annual change = after-tax interest − inflation erosion. Cumulative balance starts at $10,000 and compounds year over year. "Uninvested cash" shows $10,000 eroded by inflation alone with no interest earned. "Roth" assumes 0% tax on interest (approximating tax-free growth).
HYSA rates: Estimated 80th-pct competitive rates from FDIC data, Fed funds rate history, Bankrate/LegalClarity benchmarks. Verify at fred.stlouisfed.org.
Inflation: BLS CPI-U annual averages. Sources: U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED).
